Configure PayPal Express Checkout with Odoo

Before configuring PayPal, it's necessary to create an account on the same. Below is the list of steps to have PayPal Express up and running on your Odoo website.

PayPal Account

  • Firstly, create A PayPal Account to receive payments from the customers placing orders on your Odoo website.

Paste PayPal Client Id in Odoo

  • Once your account is created you will get a unique Client ID. Install the module in your Odoo and paste your PayPal Client ID in the form for PayPal Express checkout in Odoo.

Publish the Paypal payment method in Odoo

  • Then publish the payment method on the Odoo website to make it available for the customers.
