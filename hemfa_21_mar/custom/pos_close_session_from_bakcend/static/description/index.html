Close Session and POST entries Button


After installing the app, open POS dashboard where all shop/ restaurant are listed, you will notice a button “Close session and post entries” Click on the Button to close the current session & post journal entries.

Redirect to new Page


As soon as you will click on the button, you will redirect to new page, you can also open this page by going to Orders→ session The session is currently in the "IN PROGRESS" stage.

Close session POP up


When you click on the “Close session and post journal entry button” a pop window will open where all details like total orders, Amount, payment methods will display regarding the session, check everything and click on close session.

After Close the session


When you close the session, it moves to the "Close & Posted" stage, confirming that the session has been successfully closed, and the journal entry has been recorded.

Journal Entry


To confirm a journal entry, follow these steps: Go to the "Accounting→accounting→ journal entry.
In the search bar, enter the POS order number as the reference number (e.g., POS0006). You will then be able to view the journal entries related to closed sessions. You can also refer to the image for a visual guide. This process allows you to easily access and review journal entries associated with closed sessions by searching for them using the POS order number as a reference.
