All in one Product Weight Details -Sales, Purchase, Delivery Odoo Apps

All in One Weight Details for Sale Order, Purchase Order and Delivery Order Odoo App helps users to set weight for each product. Users can calculate the weight of the product in the order line and display total weight of the products into sale order, purchase order, delivery order, customer invoice and vendor bill.

Features

Set Weight for each Product

User can set weight for each product.

Calculate Weight of Product in Sale Order

User can calculate weight of product in sale order line and display total weight of products in sale order.

Calculate Weight of Product in Purchase Order

User can calculate weight of product in purchase order line and display total weight of products in purchase order.

Calculate Weight of Product in Delivery Order

User can calculate weight of product in delivery order line and display total weight of products in delivery order.

Calculate Weight of Product in Customer Invoice

User can calculate weight of product in customer invoice line and display total weight of products in customer invoice.

Calculate Weight of Product in Vendor Bill

User can calculate weight of product in Vendor bill line and display total weight of products in vendor bill.
