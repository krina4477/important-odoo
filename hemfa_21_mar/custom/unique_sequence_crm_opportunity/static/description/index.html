Sequence Number CRM Lead / Opportunity

Unique Sequence Number Lead / Opportunity in Odoo

This app generates a unique sequence number of lead/opportunity when it will be created.

Main Features:

  • Generate unique sequence for CRM Lead / Opportunity.
  • Sequence numbers will be displayed on tree, form, kanban, calendar, pivot, graph and activity view of opportunity / lead.
  • Sequence numbers will be displayed on the opportunity list and form a view on my account portal to your customers.
  • Please note that the sequence number of lead/opportunity is the same prefix so for both lead/opportunity sequence number will be common.
  • For more details please check below screenshots and watch the video.
