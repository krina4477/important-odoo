Account Asset Disposal in Odoo

Only for Odoo Community Edition

This module enables the feature to dispose of the asset. Users can use two below methods to dispose of any asset.

  • Sales Dispose
  • Asset Write-Off

This will allow users to dispose of the company directly from Asset form.

You can see blog of our here on this: http://www.probuse.com/blog/erp-functional-27/post/asset-disposal-write-off-69

  • **Note: This module only supports Invoice sales of Asset not Cash sales.
